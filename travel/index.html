"To travel is to discover that everyone is wrong about other countries" - Aldous Huxley

"The world is a book and those who do not travel read only one page." - Augustine of Hippo

I am fortunate to have traveled to some amazing places, but the world is so very very big and fascinating. One lifetime is far too short to fully absorb the humanity, culture, and natural beauty of our planet...but I intend to give it my best shot.

Recently I wrote about Croatia. I also posted some of my amateur travel videos.
